138-in-1 Multicart Dipswitch Mod Summary

Source: Neo-Geo Forums - Tech Support
Thread: "The Super 138 in 1 without the sub-pcb: using a dipswitch. Game list with binary code."
Date: June 2012 - December 2013

Overview

The 138-in-1 multicart originally requires a sub-PCB to function, which handles the game selection menu. This modification allows the cartridge to be used without the sub-PCB by using a dipswitch to manually select games via binary codes.

Key Advantage: The 138-in-1 contains 22 games that are NOT available on the 161-in-1 multicart, making it a valuable companion cartridge.

Important: Dipswitch Logic

CRITICAL: In the dipswitch, ON = 0 and OFF = 1. This is the opposite of what you might expect!

Wiring Information

The wires from the sub-PCB connector should be connected as follows (numbered to match selection codes):

Wire Color Function Dipswitch Position
White Ground Common
Red Voltage (+5V) Common
Green Signal 1 Position 1
Yellow Signal 2 Position 2
Brown Signal 3 Position 3
Blue Signal 4 Position 4
Orange Signal 5 Position 5
Black Signal 6 Position 6
Grey Signal 7 Position 7
Purple Signal 8 Position 8

Critical Wiring Requirement: SPDT Switches

IMPORTANT: Do NOT use simple ON/OFF switches! The multiplexer chip in the cartridge does NOT like floating inputs and will cause graphical errors and crashes.

Solution: Use SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) switches:

  • ON position: Connects signal line to Voltage (+5V)
  • OFF position: Connects signal line to Ground (NOT left floating)

This ensures that each signal line is always connected to either voltage or ground, never left floating.

Games That Work Better on 138-in-1 vs 161-in-1

Some games have issues on the 161-in-1 but work correctly on the 138-in-1:

  • Samurai Shodown II: 161 version has missing instruments in music and crackling in stage select music. Works fine on 138.
  • Garou: Mark of the Wolves: 161 has beta version with messed up ending text, different move timing, and Kim Jae Hoon's TOP move crashes. 138 has final version and works correctly.
  • Aero Fighters 3: 161 is unplayable (bullets freeze, unkillable bosses). Works fine on 138.
  • Last Blade 2: 161 can spontaneously reset. Works correctly on 138.
